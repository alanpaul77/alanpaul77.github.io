Results-driven retail & operations leader. Currently founder of PT. Cahayaera Sejahtera Sentora, a motorcycle parts distribution business in Jakarta.

My career began in sales at PT. Graha Reka Adiyasa, where I managed B2B client relationships in the glass machinery industry. I then moved into retail operations as Store Manager at PT. Lion Super Indo, overseeing a high-volume supermarket across multiple departments.

From 2005 to 2016, I served as both Regional Manager and Merchandising Manager at PT. Ramayana Lestari Sentosa Tbk — one of Indonesia's largest retail chains — managing multiple store locations, regional KPIs, and merchandising strategy for food and non-food categories across the region.

In 2016, I founded PT. Cahayaera Sejahtera Sentora, applying decades of operational discipline to build and grow a motorcycle parts distribution business from the ground up.
